AttendIQ — Smart Attendance Tracker

AttendIQ is an unofficial student project built for checking attendance data. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any college or its ERP system.

🔐 Your Credentials

Your Admission ID and Password are sent directly to our backend only to fetch your attendance from the ERP. They are never stored, logged, or shared with anyone. Sessions are cleared automatically within 5 minutes.

⚠️ Use at Your Own Risk

By logging in, you voluntarily choose to use this service. You are responsible for your own account. If the college ERP changes or flags unusual access, AttendIQ is not liable.

📊 Data Accuracy

All data is fetched directly from the ERP and displayed as-is. AttendIQ does not modify any attendance records.

🛠️ No Warranty

This is a free tool maintained by a student. It may go down, break, or stop working at any time. There is no guarantee of uptime or accuracy.

🚫 Not for Misuse

You may not use AttendIQ to access another student's data or to misrepresent your attendance in any way.

AttendIQ is not affiliated with any college.
